Overview

WH-O is seeking a Web Developer to integrate into their growing design team. This person would work with partners and designers to recommend and implement web solutions for a wide range of projects and clients. Our Web Developer should have an excellent working knowledge of computer code, as well as a strong appreciation for design and user-experience. This person will be fluent in programming HTML, CSS & Javascript, loves to learn new things and problem-solve on the job. We’re looking for someone who has impressive attention to detail, who isn’t satisfied with quick fixes or easy solutions and is eager to identify and learn from past mistakes. As a client-side engineer, this person would collaborate with project teams to realize strategic project outcomes.

What we're looking for

  • 3-5 years client-side development experience, preferably in an agency environment
  • Experience and familiarity with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • An understanding of Computer Science fundamentals including object-oriented design, data structures, algorithm design, problem solving, and complexity analysis
  • Excellent communication skills, including the ability to speak with technological fluency, while also being able to translate for non-engineers
  • Thoughtful time-management skills that allow you to juggle multiple projects as-needed
  • Problem-solving and thorough debugging skills, eager to enhance performance in previously created code
  • Collaborative, team-player who understands agency workflow and enjoys working on a variety of client projects
